Introducing myself to Ping Pong

I am a beginner to this kind of sport. When I moved to Missouri in April of 2016, I found a new love of mine: Ping Pong. It was nested in the basement of my girlfriend's parent's house, a ping pong table. It was inconspicuous, but seemed to call to me. I picked up the paddle and proceeded to badger my girlfriend for a quick rally (to hit the ball back and forth). She agreed, and several minutes later I found myself in love.
